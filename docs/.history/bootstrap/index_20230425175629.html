Grid

Here is a grid. The Bootstrap grid is 12 columns wide, and uses classes to determine how wide an element is under the circumstances.

  • <div class="row">
    <div class="co0l col-md-2 col-sm-12">     will be 2/12 of the row (about 17%) on a screen that's greater than 767px wide, and 12/12 (100%) of the row on a screen smaller than 768px wide.

  • <div class="row">
    <div class="col">Something</div>
    <div class="col">Something else</div>
    <div class="col">Something completely different</div>

    Every <div class="col"> will add another column of equal width to the row.
